FAQs

Why would my organisation need Xapo’s Institutional Offering?

The Xapo Institutional Account is a powerful financial platform built on five core pillars, enabling your organisation to manage and grow its digital assets:

  • Institutional-grade custody for your Bitcoin, ensuring top-tier security infrastructure and peace of mind.
  • A USD bank account that allows you to earn competitive yields on your USD balances.
  • Access flexible financing, enabling you to borrow USD against your Bitcoin collateral without selling your assets.
  • Direct access to the Bitcoin Credit Fund to generate an enhanced return on your Bitcoin holdings.
  • Efficient trade execution at institutional-level pricing for both your crypto and fiat orders, all within one integrated platform.

What distinguishes an Institutional Account from a Personal Account?

The Institutional Account serves legal entities and arrangements (i.e. non-natural persons) seeking strategic management of their Bitcoin and fiat reserves on their balance sheet. The Institutional platform provides online access, with the option of different user access levels for different roles, essential for organisational control. Unlike a personal account, it is not intended for a high-volume of payments, it only allows transfers of funds to and from accounts in the organisation's ‘own name’ and it does not include debit cards.

Can my organisation use the Institutional Account for operational fiat or stablecoin payments to third parties?

No. All payments to and from this account must be between your Xapo Bank account and bank accounts or wallets that share the exact same account holder name. This means you cannot send or receive funds from a third party (e.g., paying a supplier or processing payroll).

What are your typical onboarding requirements?

Our onboarding requirements adhere to standard financial industry practices. We will guide you on the specific documents needed, which vary depending on your entity's legal type. Typically, this includes: Business registration documents. Identification for key personnel (e.g., passports). Proof of the business operating address. Source of Funds (SOF) documentation (e.g., bank statements, proof of income). Company documents confirming authorised representatives.:

  • Registration / Incorporation / Establishment documents.
  • Documentation enabling the identification of key personnel.
  • Identification documents for key personnel (e.g. passports).
  • Proof of the business operating address.
  • Source of Funds (SOF) and Source of Wealth (SOW).
  • Company documents confirming authorised representatives.

How does Bitcoin custody at Xapo work?

At Xapo, we employ a world-class security architecture for your digital assets, integrating advanced technology with formidable physical protection. Our digital defenses are powered by Multi-Party Computation (MPC), an innovative technology that eliminates any single point of failure by securely splitting and distributing cryptographic keys. This digital strength is further reinforced by our unparalleled physical security, which includes a global network of secret, bomb-proof bunkers. These ultra-secure facilities are kept offline and protected by rigorous operational protocols, armed guards, and advanced surveillance.

Can I transfer assets from a personal Xapo account to an Institution Account?

We understand that transitioning from an individual to a legal entity account can be a strategic move. Based on the correct legal documentation behind this transfer it will be reviewed. We are equipped to assist with asset transfers, account setup, and the necessary compliance re-onboarding process, however, we cannot provide legal, tax or financial guidance in relation to a transfer of assets.

Are my organisation's fiat funds protected?

Yes, all corporate account fiat balances are protected under the Gibraltar Deposit Guarantee Scheme (GDGS) up to €100,000 or its equivalent in another currency.

Who is the Xapo Institutional Account for?

Xapo Bank’s Institutional Accounts provides a specialised suite of services designed for the needs of our clients:

  • Bitcoin and banking services - required by Family Offices (FOs) and Ultra-High-Net-Worth (UHNW) Companies.
  • Bitcoin-backed USD financing - required by Proprietary Trading Firms, Prime Brokers and Hedge Funds.
  • Bitcoin Treasury Account Services - required by Exchanges, Digital Asset Treasuries (DATs) and Corporates.

Can I open a Xapo Institutional Account to receive payments for my sole trader, contracting business?

Our Institutional Account may not be the ideal fit for your contracting business. It is a specialised solution for managing an organisation’s crypto and fiat funds, and its benefits are typically most realised by entities with significant holdings or borrowing needs in the millions.

How do I onboard my organisation?

Onboarding follows three steps:

  • Step 1 - To start, an authorised representative from your organisation should initiate a request by clicking on the ‘Schedule a call’ on the Xapo Bank Institution webpage
  • Step 2 - You will be asked to provide your organisation’s details and indicate the solutions you require, after which we will contact you with a list of necessary supporting documents.
  • Step 3 - Providing accurate and complete information ensures a fast onboarding process, typically completed within three weeks.


Not all applications are successful as Xapo’s Institutional Account might not be a match for your requirements for institutional banking.

Does this account support multiple users?

Yes, absolutely. The account features built-in permissions, roles, and responsibilities crucial for maintaining security and compliance. It supports dual authorisation for key transactions, while also accommodating single-user access for sole-owner entities.

Are there minimum balance requirements or a specific fee structure?

There is no minimum balance required for these accounts or wallets. An Annual Platform Fee (also payable monthly) will apply based on your specific requirements. The fee grants access to our services, often at no additional cost or at institutional-grade rates. Bitcoin custody is included in the Annual Platform Fee without percentage-based custody fees.

Do you onboard organisations with complex holding structures and multiple entities?

Yes, we are equipped to review and onboard organisations with complex holding structures on a case-by-case basis. Our primary focus is on developing a solution that meets your needs while adhering to our strict security and compliance policies. A core principle we follow is that all funds must be moved exclusively to and from accounts held in the same legal name as the verified entity, this ensures transparency and security across your transactions.

Are there country-specific restrictions for opening a corporate account?

Xapo Bank is unable to onboard institutions that are registered, operating or controlled by persons located in countries that Xapo Bank deems to be high-risk. This includes, without limitation, countries subject to applicable international sanctions or those listed on the Financial Action Task Force (FATF) blacklist.

Please note that some of our services might be restricted in certain jurisdictions.
