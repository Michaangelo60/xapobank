Multi-layered protections

Our cutting-edge security combines diverse layers of defences. Featuring a decentralised key system powered by multi-party computation (MPC) protocols and a hidden global network of physical bunkers protected by armed guards, it has kept our members’ Bitcoin holdings safe for over a decade.

A unique regulatory structure

Our status as a regulated bank and Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) means we adhere to strict regulatory standards designed to keep your funds safe. We are routinely audited by one of the big 4 accountancy firms.
